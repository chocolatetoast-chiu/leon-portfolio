About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB), specializing in the intersection of deep learning and medical imaging. My research focuses on developing AI-driven tools for brain PET/MRI analysis to better understand and diagnose neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's disease. I am passionate about translating complex imaging data into actionable clinical insights.
